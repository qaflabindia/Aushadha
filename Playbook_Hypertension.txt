1 Problem statement and what we will demonstrate
Problem Statement 3 expects an AI enabled system that helps AYUSH professionals create structured Electronic Health Records, detect early signals of local disease surges, and generate personalised treatment and lifestyle recommendations with explainability and trust. This submission demonstrates a Stage 1 Proof of Concept for hypertension using synthetic and public data only.
2 Frozen scope statement
Copy the following text exactly into every place it is needed, including portal, solution document, pitch deck, and demo script.
We will deliver a Stage 1 Proof of Concept for hypertension with three frozen use cases. Use case 1 is multilingual clinician note to structured EHR fields in Hindi and English with clinician review. Use case 2 is personalised AYUSH aligned care planning for hypertension with a Clinician Trust Layer. Use case 3 is a minimal early warning demo using aggregated synthetic counts and an alert review queue. Stage 1 uses synthetic and public data only. Stage 1 does not claim live AHMIS integration. Stage 1 does not claim real world deployment outcomes.

10 IPR and licensing compliance block
The following IPR clause is in the guidelines document. Treat it as binding for your submission and planning.
All Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) will belong to the solution owner participating in the Challenge. IndiaAI and partnering institutions shall have a non exclusive, royalty free, perpetual license to use the awarded AI solution including all IPR arising out of its use, and the solution owner shall be deemed to have given a No Objection Certificate (NOC) for the same and shall also remain bound by the terms of a Non Disclosure Agreement (NDA) with respect to such work.
What the team must do to comply
Create an internal one page acknowledgement note signed by Founder and Team Leader that the team accepts the license clause
Update ThirdPartyRegister to confirm every third party license allows this form of non exclusive use rights for an awarded solution
Decide what parts of code and documentation can be shared if asked for verification, and record it in a ShareabilityNote
Ensure the demo environment does not contain secrets, keys, or proprietary customer data
11 Employer NOC block
